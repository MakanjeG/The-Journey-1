Section 06

Strategies Playbook

Pullback-to-EMA Swing

Trending
EntryPullback to 20/50 EMA in HTF uptrend, RSI 40-50, candle confirmation.
StopBelow swing low or 1.5×ATR.
TargetTP1 prior swing high · TP2 1R extension · TP3 trail 2×ATR.
ExitTime stop at 3 candles past EMA without recovery.

Breakout-Retest

Volatility Expansion
EntryWait for breakout close, then enter on pullback retest.
StopBeyond retest extreme — 1.5×ATR minimum.
TargetMeasured move or next S/R.
ExitIf retest fails, exit at breakeven. No discretion.

Mean Reversion at VWAP

Ranging / NY
Entry±2σ from VWAP during NY with RSI divergence, fade back to VWAP.
StopBeyond ±3σ band / swing extreme.
TargetVWAP touch (TP1) · prior session high/low (TP2).

Trend-Following w/ ATR Trail

Strong Trend
EntryBOS confirmation on HTF, LTF pullback to 21EMA.
StopInitial: 1.5×ATR · Trail: 2×ATR from peak.
TargetOpen target — 25% at 1R / 25% at 2R / runner on trail.

Section 08

Trading Psychology

The market is neutral. Your state is not.

FOMO
Feels like: "It's running without me."
Costs you: Worst entries of the day.
Antidote: 5-minute away-from-screen rule.
Revenge Trading
Feels like: "Make it back NOW."
Costs you: Compounding losses.
Antidote: 30-min cooldown after any loss ≥ 1R.
Over-Trading
Feels like: "I should be in a position."
Costs you: Trades with no edge.
Antidote: 3 trades/day cap.

After-Loss Protocol

  1. Close the chart. Stand up. Do not click again.
  2. Log the trade now with at least one error tag.
  3. Wait 30 minutes before next setup evaluation.
  4. After 3 consecutive losses, halve risk for the next 5 trades.
