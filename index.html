Section 05

Strategies Playbook

Pullback-to-EMA

Trending · HTF and LTF agree direction · ADX > 20 · entry at 21 EMA pullback.

Breakout-Retest

Vol Expansion · trigger: candle breaks pivot, retests, entry at retest.

Mean-Reversion at 21 EMA

Ranging · stoch extreme + price >0.8% from EMA · fade back to mean.

Order-Block

Any regime · small-body OB candle + displacement candle · enter on OB close.

BOS-Continuation

Strong trend · BOS + inside-bar pullback · enter on inside-bar break.

Section 07

Trading Psychology

😰 FOMO
Feels like: "It's running without me."
Antidote: 5-minute away-from-screen rule.
😡 Revenge Trading
Feels like: "Make it back NOW."
Antidote: 30-min cooldown after any loss ≥ 1R.
🎰 Over-Trading
Feels like: "I should be in a position."
Antidote: 3 trades/day cap.

After-Loss Protocol

  1. Close the chart. Stand up. Do not click again.
  2. Log the trade now with at least one error tag.
  3. Wait 30 minutes before next setup evaluation.
  4. After 3 consecutive losses, halve risk for the next 5 trades.
